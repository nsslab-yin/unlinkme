Heap Double Free :: Unlinkme

  • In this lab, students can get first-hand experience on exploiting heap-based double free vulnerability.
  • By playing with the stepwise challenges, students can understand how the heap is managed by glibc malloc() and learn to exploit the unlink() after figuring out double free vulnerabilities.
